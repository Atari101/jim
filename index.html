About Me

Welcome! My name is Jim. I’m from Wisconsin, the home of cheese, beer and the Green Bay Packers. I’ve been doing carpentry for over ten years. One of the things I most enjoy about carpentry is being able to take a vision in my head and bring it to life for others to enjoy. I only use the finest pieces of wood for quality products. Please check out some of my work below and read testimonials from my clients. It would be my pleasure to custom design a product for you. Send me an e-mail by clicking the link at the bottom and I'll get in touch as soon as I can. Thanks so much for stopping by!
